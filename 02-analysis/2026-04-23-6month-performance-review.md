## Key Signals

### 🟢 What's working
- **6-month blended ROAS (8.96x) and CPO ($19.84) are both on target** when viewed across the full period.
- **January and February were the benchmark months** — ROAS 8.9x / 8.5x, CPO $21.30 / $22.21. These are the numbers to aim to sustain.
- **April (partial) is tracking as the strongest month of 2026** — ROAS 9.51x, CPO $21.86. Snapchat contributing for the first time ($24K, 105 purchases).
- **Meta is consistently strong** — ROAS 9.1x–9.9x in Jan–Apr with stable CPO. Growing from 16% to ~40% of spend is a correct call.

### 🔴 What needs attention
- **GA4 ME revenue has declined every month since January** — $672K → $540K → $434K → $343K (partial). Sessions also dipped in April to 264K. This is the most important signal to investigate — could be seasonal, product/inventory, or attribution-related.
- **Google ROAS degraded in March to 6.67x** (below target) despite flat spend. Conversion count dropped from 1,283 (Jan) to 966 (Mar). Something changed in campaign efficiency — worth auditing match types, audience overlap, or bidding strategy.
- **CPO above $22 in 3 of 7 months** (Nov, Dec, Mar). March is the key concern — highest spend yet worst CPO.

### ⚠️ Data caveats
- **October Google anomaly** — 3,037 conversions at 15x ROAS on $29.8K spend is atypically high. Likely reflects a wide attribution window or early-stage campaign setup. Excluding October, realistic blended ROAS ≈ 7.9x.
- **Shopify timed out** — GA4 ME used as revenue ground truth. Cross-check with Shopify Admin for full accuracy.
- **April is partial** (22/30 days) — all April figures will improve by month close.

## Top 3 Actions

1. 🔴 **Investigate January→April revenue decline** — GA4 ME revenue is down 49% from the Jan peak. Check if this is a product availability issue, a drop in organic/direct traffic, or a paid channel efficiency problem. Pull GA4 by channel to isolate.
2. ⚠️ **Audit Google March performance** — ROAS fell to 6.67x and CPO hit $27.70. Compare campaign-level performance Jan vs Mar. Check if budget was reallocated to lower-performing campaigns or if ROAS bidding targets were loosened.
3. 🚀 **Scale Snapchat if April holds** — 105 purchases at $24K revenue on only $950 spend implies strong ROAS. Validate attribution window and test increasing budget in May.
