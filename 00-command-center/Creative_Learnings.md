## Creative Testing Rules

- Test one main angle at a time when possible.
- Separate product angle, offer angle, and format angle in naming.
- Judge early tests by CPO, CTR, thumbstop, and add-to-cart quality, not ROAS alone.
- Promote winners into broader campaigns only after the signal is stable.
- Kill or rewrite creative that spends enough to show weak CTR and weak purchase intent.
