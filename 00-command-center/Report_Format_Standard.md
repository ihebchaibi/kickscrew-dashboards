# Report Format Standard

Source format: Slack Canvas `KICKS CREW ADS ME - Weekly Report`, pulled from Slack on 2026-05-03.

Canvas ID: `F0B0H3D4TG9`

## Default Report Order

Use this order for daily, weekly, and monthly reports:

1. Title and report period.
2. Published date and sources.
3. Verdict headline with blended ROAS and CPO.
4. Short verdict paragraph.
5. #1 issue.
6. At-a-glance table.
7. Channel sections.
8. Shopify ME ground truth.
9. GA4 ME funnel.
10. Revenue cross-check triangle.
11. Top 3 actions.

## Standard Sources

- Google Ads
- Meta Ads
- Snapchat Ads
- TikTok Ads when active
- GA4 ME
- Shopify via Windsor.ai
- Manual Shopify Admin reconciliation when needed

## Standard Targets

| Metric | Target |
| --- | --- |
| Blended ROAS | 7x healthy floor |
| CPO | $22 healthy threshold |
| AOV | $175+ |
| Attribution gap | Under 20% preferred |

## Status Colors

Use these labels consistently:

- Green: meets target.
- Yellow: near target or needs attention.
- Red: materially below target or requires action.
- Warning: CPO above threshold, ROAS below floor, or data issue.

## Channel Section Format

Each paid channel should use:

`Channel - $X spend | ROAS X.xx | CPO/CPP $X`

Then a campaign table:

| Campaign | Spend | Revenue | ROAS | CPO/CPP | Purchases/Conv |
| --- | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: |

## Action Format

Top 3 actions should be direct and decision-ready:

1. Action.
2. Why.
3. Owner or next step.

## Daily vs Weekly vs Monthly

Daily reports should be shorter and focus on yesterday/today movement, broken campaigns, and immediate actions.

Weekly reports should follow the full Canvas format and include WoW changes.

Monthly reports should follow the full Canvas format plus OKR pacing, budget deployment, and month-level strategic learnings.
