**Published:** May 3, 2026 · **Sources:** Google Ads + Meta Ads + Snapchat Ads + GA4 ME + Shopify (Windsor.ai) · **Format:** Slack Canvas weekly report standard · **April Shopify:** reconciled to Admin · **March caveat:** March comparison uses the 6-month review baseline, where Shopify timed out and GA4 ME was used as ground-truth context.

---

## Monthly Verdict - Blended ROAS 9.85x Green | CPO $21.06 Green

April was a clear efficiency recovery after March. Spend increased from $47.1K to $60.9K, but revenue grew much faster, from $358.8K to $600.1K. That lifted blended ROAS from 7.62x to 9.85x and brought CPO down from $25.58 to $21.06, moving the account back under the $22 healthy threshold. The biggest improvement was not just higher spend; it was better budget quality, led by AE PMax, Meta catalog/product-burst campaigns, and Snapchat finally producing measurable revenue.

---

## #1 Issue - Budget Scale Gap: $137K underspend vs April OKR model

April proved the account can run efficiently, but it did not deploy enough budget to hit the monthly GMV target. Blended ROAS was healthy at 9.85x and CPO was controlled at $21.06, yet spend reached only $60.9K, about 31% of the $197.5K April OKR budget model. The next problem is not "can we make paid media work?" The problem is "where can we safely scale without breaking CPO?" The best scale candidates are Snapchat KSA, Pokémon-style Meta bursts, AE PMax, and the cleaner post-split Meta UAE lane.

## What We Did Better in April

1. We moved from a March efficiency problem to an April scale problem. March CPO was $25.58; April CPO improved to $21.06 even with higher spend.
2. We launched and proved new scale lanes: AE PMax, Snapchat KSA/UAE, and Pokémon-style product bursts.
3. We split Meta tests into clearer market logic. UAE ASC worked; KSA/GCC/EG did not. That is still progress because the decision is now clear.
4. We improved reporting quality. April has Shopify reconciled to Admin, GA4 cross-check, and a repeatable Canvas-style monthly structure.
5. We protected AOV. April AOV was $184.96, above the $175 target, so the account did not buy cheap orders at the expense of order quality.

## Top 3 Actions for Ihab - May 2026

1. **Scale the proven winners, not the whole account.** Increase Snapchat KSA, AE PMax, and Pokémon-style Meta bursts first because they combine high ROAS with strong CPO. Keep increments controlled and review CPO every 48-72 hours.
2. **Cut or rebuild weak post-split Meta lanes.** Pause or rebuild 2604_ASC_KSA, 2604_ASC_GCC, 2604_ASC_EG, and 2604_KSA_manual. Keep the split architecture, but change the creative and market assumptions where the data says they failed.
3. **Fix Google drag before adding more spend.** SA Shop Conversion and KU PMax are the main Google leaks. Audit feed quality, bidding, and search/product mix, then move budget into AE PMax and SA PMax until CPO normalizes.
