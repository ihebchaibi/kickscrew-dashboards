🏆 Key Wins
🎯

14.3% CTR — Exceptional Click-Through

Industry average for Meta ads sits around 0.9–1.5%. Airselli achieved a 14.3% CTR, meaning nearly 1 in 7 people who saw the ads clicked — driven by the Airselli Messaging Compaigne 1 (43K clicks from 152K views).

💰

€159 for 667K Impressions — $0.23 CPM

Global Meta CPMs average €5–€12. Airselli generated 667,582 impressions for €159 — a CPM of just €0.24. Every euro of budget delivered well above market value in visibility.

❤️

وش هو Airselli؟ 2.0 — Star Creative

The brand video drove 8,029 engagements with 7,649 reactions — a 95% reaction rate. For €26.26 on 21K viewers, this is the most efficient brand awareness creative of the month.

👍

1,154 New Page Followers at €0.15 Each

The dedicated Page Likes campaign (Apr 26) delivered 1,154 new followers for €17.48 — a cost per follower of just €0.015. Exceptional community growth at minimal cost.

📩

3 Active Campaigns Still Running

Messaging Compaigne 2.0 Lookalike, Page Likes, and Airselli Messaging Compaigne 1 are all still active as of May 1 — results will continue to accumulate. Current trajectory is very strong.

⚡

16.7% Engagement Rate — 5× Benchmark

The blended engagement rate of 16.7% across all 8 campaigns is roughly 5× the Meta paid benchmark. The delivery-focused "3 خطوات فقط" post stands out with 39,828 engagements from 102K views.
