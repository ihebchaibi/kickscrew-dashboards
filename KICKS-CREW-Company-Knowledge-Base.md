## 3. Business Model

KICKS CREW operates a **hybrid marketplace**: the storefront carries both house-sourced inventory (via CrewSupply) and listings from numbered third-party sellers who ship via the KICKS CREW logistics backbone. Authenticity is the moat — centralized authentication in HK before fulfillment.

### Two engines
- **Marketplace storefront** — `kickscrewshop.myshopify.com` (Shopify Plus), plus marketplace integrations (noon, namshi, ArabyAds in ME; Zalando/eBay-style plays in other regions as surfaced).
- **CrewSupply** — supply-sourcing arm. Procurement hubs in HK, Korea, Japan, Taiwan, US. Feeds both owned inventory and wholesale flows.

### Monetization layers
1. Direct D2C sneaker + apparel + accessories sales (margin + velocity).
2. Marketplace take-rate on 3P seller orders.
3. Trading cards (Pokémon launched 2025; NBA cards in scope) — high-margin, low-return vertical.
4. Partnerships & brand collabs (ANTA partnership active; Kyrie Irving IP/community halo).

### Fulfillment & logistics
- Core fulfillment from HK/Asia hubs → regional last-mile.
- ME last-mile via Easyship (and local partners); Klarna enabled for installments in select markets.

## 12. Data Rules & Gotchas (reporting hygiene)

### Currency conversions
- **Google Ads** `spend` = HKD → **÷ 7.80** for USD. `conversion_value` = HKD → **÷ 8.122** for USD. `Cost_usd` field returns null — DO NOT use it.
- **Meta, Snapchat, GA4, Shopify** = native USD. No conversion.
- **Shopify `order_gross_sales`** = native USD (unusual — Shopify default is store currency, but this account returns USD).

### Filters that bite
- Shopify: `is_canceled_order` field does NOT exist in Windsor — use `["order_cancelled_at","null",null]`.
- GA4: must filter to ME countries only — never pull global GA4 as ME.
- Meta revenue: use `action_values_purchase`, not other revenue fields.

### Reconciliation contract
- Windsor.ai ≠ ground truth. Shopify Admin report = ground truth.
- If Windsor differs from Admin by > 2 orders or > 2% revenue → investigate before publish. Do not ship unreconciled reports.
