## 🚨 Critical Assessment — Are These Targets Realistic?

### Why the OKR is Structurally Unachievable in FY26

**1. Ad spend is 70% underfunded.** To reach 13.5x ROAS at $2.67M/month GMV, you need ~$197K/month in spend. We're projecting ~$60K/month — one-third of what's required. Even if you tripled spend tomorrow, you'd still be short.

**2. Even with tripled budget, you won't hit OKR.** At 9.6x ROAS (current efficiency) with $197K/month spend, revenue would be ~$1.89M/month — still 29% short of the $2.67M target. And ROAS typically drops as you scale spend aggressively into less efficient audiences.

**3. The targets assume ~480% YoY growth.** The global platform is growing at +1.06% YoY (April data). Even if ME grows 5x faster than the company average, that's ~5% growth. Getting to 480% requires structural demand that isn't there yet.

**4. This was set without a baseline.** Julien confirmed "we have no prior data." The targets appear to be aspirational/strategic (ME is a key OKR pillar) rather than data-grounded.

### What IS Achievable in FY26

| Scenario | Monthly GMV | Annual GMV | Orders | Budget |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Status quo** (no change) | ~$456K | ~$5.5M | ~29K | ~$60K/mo |
| **2x spend** (~$120K/mo) | ~$870K | ~$10.4M | ~55K | $120K/mo |
| **3x spend** (~$180K/mo) | ~$1.3M | ~$15.6M | ~83K | $180K/mo |
| **OKR target** | $2,667K | $32M | 171K | $197K/mo |

The 3x spend scenario is the most aggressive realistic push for FY26. It gets ME to ~$15-16M GMV annually — about **50% of OKR**, which is still a strong story given there was no ME baseline.

### One Bright Spot

**AOV at $187 beats the $175 target.** This means the product mix and pricing strategy in ME is working. The problem is pure volume, not unit economics.
