Monthly Verdict ROAS 9.85x CPO $21.06

April was a clear efficiency recovery after March. Spend increased from $47.1K to $60.9K, but revenue grew much faster, from $358.8K to $600.1K. That lifted blended ROAS from 7.62x to 9.85x and brought CPO down from $25.58 to $21.06, moving the account back under the $22 healthy threshold.

#1 Issue Budget Scale Gap

April proved the account can run efficiently, but it deployed only $60.9K, around 31% of the $197.5K April OKR budget model. The next problem is not whether paid media works; it is where we can safely scale without breaking CPO.
