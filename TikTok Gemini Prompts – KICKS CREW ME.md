## HOW TO USE THESE PROMPTS

1. Open **gemini.google.com** → select **Gemini 2.0 Flash** or **Gemini Advanced**
2. For images: switch to **image generation mode** and paste the image prompt
3. For videos: use **Veo 2** (available in Gemini Advanced) and paste the video prompt
4. Download at highest resolution available
5. For images: import into **CapCut** or **TikTok's Creative Center** and add text overlays per the ad copy sheet
6. For videos: edit in CapCut — add text overlays, CTA cards, and music from TikTok Commercial Library
7. Export at 1080×1920, H.264, minimum 30fps
